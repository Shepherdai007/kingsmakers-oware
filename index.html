How to Play Oware
1
The board has 2 rows of 6 pits. Each starts with 4 seeds. You own the bottom row.
2
On your turn, pick any of your pits with seeds and distribute them one by one counter-clockwise.
3
Capture: If your last seed lands in an opponent's pit making it exactly 2 or 3, you capture those seeds!
4
Captures continue backwards if preceding pits also have 2 or 3 seeds.
5
If a move would leave your opponent with no seeds, you must choose another move (grand slam rule).
6
Game ends when a player has 25+ seeds or no valid moves exist. Most seeds wins!
